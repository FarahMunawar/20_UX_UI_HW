Farah Munawar

Redesign Greyhound Trust website presents a contemporary and trustworthy design, delivering succinct and factual details, streamlining the adoption process, and facilitating efficient communication with the charity.

OUR SOLUTION

A modern, trustworthy website with a high-quality design that provides concise and factual information, a simple adoption process and effective contact with the charity.

THE PROBLEM

The ‘Greyhound Trust for Merseyside and Cheshire’ site lacks high quality pictures and sufficient information, making it difficult to understand the adoption process and find the right Greyhound.

RESEARCH STRATEGY

To find out issues with the usability of the current Mersey & Cheshire greyhound trust,
which features are important to users rehoming retired racing greyhounds.

Heuristic Evaluation & Annotation

A comprehensive heuristic evaluation including annotating the existing website.This helped, highlight issues with the UI such as a lack of hero image and footer, a confusing navigation bar, a small, serif font and text misalignment.This provides with guidance for user testing.

USER INSIGHTS

Michael needs to find a website where he can adopt and rehome a greyhound.  He is finding it difficult to trust the greyhound trust website for his local area because of its outdated design and confusing navigation.

During user interviews and surveys, we discovered that users did not trust the website due the design and functionality.  Users found that the website looked outdated, which also encouraged them to question whether the charity was still running. Therefore, I believe that a re-design will help users gain trust in the website, enabling engagement with the charity, to successfully complete an adoption.

We might do this by re designing features on the website and mobile app, to make it more trustworthy. Doing this will allow our product to be successful for users and the charity.

2

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Research found the website contained low quality images, an inconsistent colour scheme and a lack of standard website navigation header and footer features. This led users to lose trust in the website, which resulted in the charity losing sponsors. Users are seeking a trustworthy website with a high-quality design that provides concise and factual information, a simple adoption process and effective contact with the charity

VALUE PROPOSITION

Greyhound Trust Mersey & Cheshire is the trusted destination for Greyhound adoption. Our website offers expert guidance, and comprehensive resources to ensure a seamless adoption process. Join our vibrant communityof Greyhound lovers, connect with like-minded individuals, and gain access to invaluable support. Adopt a greyhound and become part of our family today.

FUTURE CONSIDERATIONS

Due to time constraints we had to prioritise features based on high priority for users and high priority for the charity as we were Redesigning the UI. But there were many other features that we would like to develop in the future that were raised from research

  • Online application form to enable customers to apply to adopt a dog online.
  • Adding racing names so prospective adopters can look into the history of the greyhound.
  • Adding time in kennels so prospective adopters know which dogs have been in the kennels the longest.
  • Events calendar for local events run by the charity.
  • Resources and educational material (general greyhound advice and  advice specific to the dog looking for a home).
  • Onboarding to save questionnaire and favourite greyhound
