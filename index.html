Farah Munawar

Redesign Greyhound Trust website presents a contemporary and trustworthy design, delivering succinct and factual details, streamlining the adoption process, and facilitating efficient communication with the charity.

OUR SOLUTION

A modern, trustworthy website with a high-quality design that provides concise and factual information, a simple adoption process and effective contact with the charity.

THE PROBLEM

The ‘Greyhound Trust for Merseyside and Cheshire’ site lacks high quality pictures and sufficient information, making it difficult to understand the adoption process and find the right Greyhound.

RESEARCH STRATEGY

To find out issues with the usability of the current Mersey & Cheshire greyhound trust,
which features are important to users rehoming retired racing greyhounds.

Heuristic Evaluation & Annotation

A comprehensive heuristic evaluation including annotating the existing website.This helped, highlight issues with the UI such as a lack of hero image and footer, a confusing navigation bar, a small, serif font and text misalignment.This provides with guidance for user testing.

1

USABILITY TESTING AND SURVEY FINDINGS

Usability Testing

Carried out focus group usability testing
with 6 participants.

Survey

Surveyed 47 retired racing greyhound owners,
including an owner of a greyhound charity.

98%
of respondents thought it was important for the website to provide comprehensive information about greyhound characteristics, behaviour, and specific care needs.

94%
of respondents thought seeing the registered charity number was essential to make the website look trustworthy.

“I need to know what conditions the charity  wants prospective adopters to be able to fill”
